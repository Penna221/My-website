Post 30.6.2024

This post is after the Hike. After this text you can see a few photos taken by Olli Pesonen.

The trip was a success! I really enjoyed it. My training paid off. Our group had 8 members. In the group photo you can see from left to right, Olli, Joona, Hannes, Olli, Salla, me, Elias, Joona. We were on top of the Kuivi mountain.

This was my very first hiking trip. I will perhaps remember this for the rest of my life. I would like to thank everyone in our group for a succesfull trip.

  2. Njávgoaivi
  3. Kuivi
